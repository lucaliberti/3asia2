Fashion & Shopping


Milan and shopping, a world famous duo. From the luxury of the fashion district to offers for all budgets on streets such as Corso Vittorio Emanuele II or Corso Buenos Aires, Milan is the city for you.

Milan is fashion and it is in its history, in its frenetic present and in its glittering future. Shiny and eclectic, always one step ahead but strong from the great names of international luxury: Milan is the capital of Italian fashion but not only.

Everyone loves it because among these iconic streets every object becomes more glamorous, every perfume knows about your next purchase and every outfit becomes an opportunity to feel part of the fashion system. The Via della Spiga, which includes Via Montenapoleone, Via Manzoni and the beginning of Corso Venezia, the quadrilateral of fashion is the wonderland of lovers of international brands. The internationality of Milan is breathed throughout the year but there are two moments in which the city becomes the absolute protagonist of the international fashion panorama: the Fashion Weeks. Models from all over the world, famous and emerging designers, fashion addicts and journalists meet in the city to attend the week.

The shopping streets such as Corso Buenos Aires, a young dynamic and within everyone's reach. Via Torino, capable of combining classic and modern, will take you beyond the Columns of San Lorenzo, where the more alternative soul so loved by millennials lives.

EVENTS

From 14 to 18 January 2022, the men's fashion shows dedicated to autumn / winter 2022/2023 will be held and from 22 to 28 February 2022, the Milan Fashion Week dedicated to women's fashion, a new major international event that combines creativity, work and beauty.

This edition includes a calendar full of projects and events that confirm the key role of Milan among the world fashion capitals, with great attention to the issues of sustainability, ethics and circular economy within the fashion supply chain. Also this year there will be the opportunity to follow the event on milanofashionweek.cameramoda.it

There will also be the opportunity to watch the fashion shows in live streaming from the big screen in Piazza San Babila, in the heart of the Milanese Fashion District.

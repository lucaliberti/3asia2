WHAT NOT TO DO IN MILAN

There are many things to do in Milan but there are also activities that are not recommended. Here is a list of 10 things not to do in Milan.

  1. Do not go into the underground tunnels: it is forbidden and will be punished by the law
  2. Do not cross the yellow line on the metro as it can be dangerous
  3. Dressing well in Milan is a sacred thing
  4. Do not go shopping in the center as it is expensive
  5. Do not walk distracted because in Milan you cannot let your guard down
  6. Do not stand in front of the opening of the metro doors if you do not want to be insulted
  7. Don't lose your metro ticket or you can't use the metro
  8. Do not soil the city that hosts you
  9. Do not go slowly by car in Milan otherwise they ring and you are insulted
  10. Milan can be a dangerous place if unaccompanied at night

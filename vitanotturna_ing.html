MILAN NIGHTLIFE

ZONE E DISCOTECHE

At the center of the city map we find Brera: the district of the famous Academy, the beautiful Pinacoteca, the artists' district. This area has always been the meeting point of famous people from the world of culture, fashion and entertainment. Even today, there are places with an international and bohemian atmosphere.

The Club:this is the Club area,very famous place that hosts the evenings of Milan Fashion Week, it hosts world-famous DJs and artists.The Club is located in Corso Giuseppe Garibaldi. Admission costs around 20 euros and includes a free drink.

CORSO SEMPIONE

This is the area frequented especially by young people in career, executives and professionals; also famous for their excellent aperitifs prepared by experienced barmen and for their music always in step with the times!

Old Fashion Cafè :Among the most famous places that can host this area, it cannot be mentioned the Old Fashion Cafè in Viale Emilio Alemagna.The style of the music changes according to the evening: on Wednesdays they organize the university evening, one of the most important events dedicated to Milan students and Erasmus students.The evening begins with an event dedicated exclusively to foreign students , which consists of an ethnic buffet, and the night then continues normally.Admission is free for women while boys pay 10 euros (before 00.30).

Gattopardo Cafè:Another nightclub in the area is the Gattopardo Cafè, in Via Piero della Francesca 47.The peculiarity of this nightclub is that it is located in an old church now deconsecrated. It is an elegant place to spend a nice evening. The prices are a bit higher than in the other clubs. Tuesday evening is dedicated to university students.An aperitif is possible until 10pm. The entry price is 10 euros.

CORSO COMO E GARIBALDI

Nearby Corso Como and in the renovated Garibaldi area. New skyscrapers, lights, squares completely transformed in style and use as the famous Piazza Gae Aulenti. Here the clubs are even more trendy, the most fashionable clubs welcome the many characters of the show that regularly frequent this area. Refinement, refinement and glamour are the adjectives that best describe this part of Milan.

Magazzini Generali: The Magazzini Generali in Via Pietrasanta 16 is one of the most famous nightclubs of the center. Characterized by an excellent sound system, it not only hosts Italian and international DJs, but also events such as concerts attended by thousands of people.

Tocqueville 13: Another very famous nightclub is Tocqueville, in Via Alexis De Tocqueville 13.The cost is 20 euros for evening with a drink included. It isn’t only a nightclub but it is also a restaurant and bar.

ISOLA

Isola: the old working-class district that a skilful make-up intervention has transformed into an alternative and very attractive environment, while keeping intact all its Milan. With a balance between tradition and ethnicity, this neighborhood is known for its jazz. One place above all is even called the Temple of Jazz and is the Blue Note, with a prestigious program that includes the most famous national and international artists.

Alcatraz: Located in Via Valtellina 21/27, this nightclub is very informal and spacious. It hosts a live music performance before the evening that starts after midnight, often a performance of cover bands or revival music.The price depends on the time of entry, before midnight it is 8 euros with a drink for women and 12 for men.The Alcatraz is also home to numerous concerts by international and Italian guests and, in conjunction with these events, the ticket is proportionate to the fame of the guest.

PORTA ROMANA

Going south we reach Porta Romana to spend the evening in the company of other musical genres: rock, ambient, 80s style. Popular themed rooms with interesting furnishings and exclusive environments for those who want to experience a late-romantic evening. In this area excellent restaurants will satisfy your palate, as well as typical bars symbol of Milano da Bere, whose characteristic is to take the drink at the counter and drink it outside the bar, chatting with friends.

Known for the presence of numerous Rock and 80s clubs. Here you will find very quiet places with rock and 80s music. They are places usually frequented by more adult people.

NAVIGLI

Navigli area, the heart of Milan’s nightlife. Young and old animate the dozens of bars, pubs, pubs, pubs, discos that all year round offer their customers cocktails, dishes and music of quality and fun. And dine along the canal? Very possible. Excellent restaurants overlook the walks that line the canals of the city. The restaurants in the area offer traditional menus but there are also restaurants with innovative and tasty proposals.

An unmissable place in this area is the Sacristia Pharmacy Alcoholic, where you can spend a full evening. The interior of the restaurant belongs to a disused pharmacy.

COLONNE DI SAN LORENZO

"See you at the Columns": so it is said among young people when you want to find yourself between Piazza Vetra and Via Molino delle Armi to chat with friends, outside the premises with a beer in hand, standing or sitting on the ground in a historical context of great value. We are in fact among the most famous Columns of Milan, one of the important Roman traces that survived at the time in front of the famous Basilica of San Lorenzo. Symbiosis between ancient and current. Local profusion, the most varied, whose offer is suggested by a young world, unconventional and colorful. Even here a local has become over time an institution. This is the Bar Rattazzo, famous for being a meeting place of the Milanese intelligentsia left in the 70s, but even more so for its excellent meatballs.

Located in Via Molino delle Armi at the corner of Via della Chiusa, the Tom is a disco pub suitable for those who want to sip an excellent drink and take advantage of the opportunity to have an aperitif with a rich and cheap buffet with their friends. The music is often alternative and not very commercial, and the evening goes on until 3 in the morning.

LAMBRATE

There is a whole different air in the Lambrate area. Here you go to the realm of beer! There is something for all tastes: blondes, redheads, dark, special, in any brewery you decide to enter you will have the guarantee of the product. As in every area, we find the historic place par excellence, a small pub known for its British atmosphere and for the excellent craft beers: the Lambrate Brewery. Refined dinner and after dinner or more easy are available for anyone who wants to spend a relaxed and quiet evening.

This area is known for its many breweries and pubs suitable for everyone. The obligatory stop for those heading to the Lambrate areas is certainly its famous brewery, which offers its customers the best craft beers.
